Meet Peter Kimani

Born and raised in Nakuru, my journey into craftsmanship began with a deep respect for raw materials and structural beauty. Known by my alias SP Gypsum, I have spent over half a decade refining the art of interior space finishing.

I specialize in the perfect blend of sturdy carpentry and delicate gypsum artistry. From the vibrant streets of Nakuru to the premium estates along Kiambu Road, my work stands as a testament to quality, durability, and modern design.
